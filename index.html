Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, products, or methods that bring value and solve problems in unique ways.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the basic physical and organizational structures, like roads, bridges, and utilities, needed for the functioning of a society or economy.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of gaining confidence, skills, and control to make decisions and take actions that positively impact one's life.
