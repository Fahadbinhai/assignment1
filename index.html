Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, products, or methods that bring value and solve problems in unique ways.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the basic physical and organizational structures, like roads, bridges, and utilities, needed for the functioning of a society or economy.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of gaining confidence, skills, and control to make decisions and take actions that positively impact one's life.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation is the integration of digital technologies into all areas of a business, fundamentally changing how it operates and delivers.

Green Energy

Green energy refers to renewable and environmentally friendly energy sources like solar, wind, and hydro power that reduce pollution and conserve natural resources.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure refers to advanced systems and facilities like transportation, communication, energy, and technology that support efficient and sustainable development.
